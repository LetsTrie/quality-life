Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mental health services

How do the assessments work?

Our assessments are scientifically designed questionnaires that evaluate different aspects of your mental well-being. They take about 15-20 minutes to complete, and you'll receive immediate insights and recommendations.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality and use bank-level encryption to protect your data. Your privacy is our top priority, and we comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

Can I speak with a professional?

Yes, we offer connections to licensed mental health professionals. After your assessment, you can schedule a consultation with a therapist who matches your needs and preferences.

How much does it cost?

We offer different plans to suit various needs. The initial assessment is free, and our subscription plans start at $29/month. We also work with several insurance providers.

How quickly can I get started?

You can start immediately! Sign up takes less than 2 minutes, and you can begin your first assessment right away. Support resources are instantly accessible upon registration.

What if I need immediate help?

If you're experiencing a crisis, we provide direct connections to 24/7 crisis hotlines and emergency services. Your safety is our primary concern.
